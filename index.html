How It Works

FOOD SUBSIDIES STROKE SATISFACTION

We offer a company-subsidized meal plan that helps your employees to get their proper nutrition. Not only it will help them to perform better at their jobs, but it also maximizes employee's morale, loyalty and improves workforce productivity.

ORDER YOUR FOOD ONLINE

Once your company has signed up for our meal services, full-time employees can order their food online 24 hours before, and they will receive a text message on their phone to confirm their order.

WE DELIVER

Our team members will deliver your order to your job site, and you will never have to stress about what's for lunch again.
